Most Well Known Kinds of Penguins

Some of the most well known penguins include the Emperor penguin,African penguin, Galapagos penguin, and the King penguin.

Emperor Penguins

This penguin is the largest in terms of mass and weight.

African Penguin

These penguins are a banded group of penguins,sticking together. Also known as black-foot penguins.

Galapagos Penguins

These penguins are also a part of the banded group. They are the least noisy of all penguins.

King Penguins

This species of penguin is the second largest after the Emeperor penguin.
